A beautiful and relaxing Chrome extension that transforms your new tab into a serene koi pond with swimming fish, lily pads, and customizable widgets.

## Features

### Interactive Koi Pond
- **Realistic koi fish** with smooth swimming animations and natural behaviors
- **Lily pads** that float on the water surface with blooming flowers
- **Dragonflies** that hover around the pond
- **Interactive feeding** - click to feed the koi and watch them swim to the food
- **Ripple effects** when interacting with the water
- **Customizable fish count** (0-50 koi) and lily pad count (0-20)

### Beautiful Customization
- **Time-of-day themes** - Watch the pond change from dawn to dusk to night
- **Custom backgrounds** - Upload your own images or use the default serene pond
- **Water color customization** - Adjust the pond water tint to your preference
- **Auto time sync** - Automatically match the pond theme to your current time
- **Multiple widget styles** - Clear Glass, Vintage Paper, Transparent, or Lily Pad themes

### Useful Widgets
All widgets are draggable, resizable, and optional:

- **Clock Widget**
  - 12/24-hour format
  - Multiple timezone support
  - Clean, readable display

- **Weather Widget**
  - Real-time weather updates
  - Automatic geolocation or manual location entry
  - Temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit
  - Powered by Open-Meteo API

- **Quote Widget**
  - Inspiring daily quotes
  - Display/hide author names
  - Add your own custom quotes

- **Search Widget**
  - Quick Google search access
  - Clean, minimal interface

### Performance
- **Performance Mode** - Reduce visual effects for lower-end devices
- **Optimized rendering** - Smooth 60 FPS animations
- **Debug overlay** - Monitor FPS and performance metrics (for advanced users)

## Known Issues & Troubleshooting

**Weather widget not showing location:**
- Ensure you've granted geolocation permission
- Try entering a location manually in weather settings

**Performance issues:**
- Enable "Performance Mode" in settings
- Reduce the number of koi fish and lily pads
- Disable unused widgets

**Widgets not saving position:**
- Check that the extension has storage permissions
- Try disabling and re-enabling the extension
